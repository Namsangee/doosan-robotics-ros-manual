Cookie Policy
-------------

Doosan Robotics (“Doosan”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy.  
We are based in the Republic of Korea and, for the purposes of the GDPR, we are the data controller.  
For inquiries, contact us via email: <marketing.robotics@doosan.com>

This Cookie Policy explains our use of cookies and social media plugins on our Website (www.doosanrobotics.com).

Cookies are small text files placed on your device that allow us to understand usage,  
analyze performance, and personalize settings.  
Some cookies are ours or from service providers; others are third-party cookies.

We also use social media plugins provided by platforms such as YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, and LinkedIn.  
These plugins may collect personal data based on your interaction.

For general data protection details outside of cookie usage, please refer to the Doosan Data Protection Policy.  
For recruitment-related data, refer to the Applicant Data Protection Policy.
